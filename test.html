About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a top-down survival shooter where you battle increasingly dangerous monster waves. Collect experience gems to unlock powerful upgrades and special abilities. Features procedurally generated maps and endless gameplay modes.

  • Intuitive touch/mouse controls
  • 20+ unique character upgrades
  • 15+ different monster types
  • Daily challenges & leaderboards
  • Regular content updates
